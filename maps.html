Fremont Brewing Company

AKA the place with the goddamn pretzels. Every day after work, I drive home. I am exhausted from trying to not allow 13, 14, and 45 year olds to run into the various bridges in Seattle. I ride past this brewery, and see all the people with more fun lives than mine sitting out enjoying themselves, and eating these soft pretzels. Every day I want to stop, but have to get home or else I will be stuck in the merciless trap of rush hour. I hope one day I will get to stop.

by Emma Delucchi 2018
